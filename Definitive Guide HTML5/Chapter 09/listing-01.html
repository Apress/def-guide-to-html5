I like apples and oranges. I also like bananas, mangoes, cherries, apricots, plums, peaches and grapes. You can see other fruits I like here. Warning: Eating too many oranges can give you heart burn. My favorite kind of orange is the mandarin, properly known as citrus reticulata. Oranges at my local store cost $1 each $2 for 3. The FDOC regulates the Florida citrus industry. I still remember the best apple I ever tasted. I bought it at 3 o'clock on December 7th.
